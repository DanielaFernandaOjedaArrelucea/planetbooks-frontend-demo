Our Story

PlanetBooks was founded in 2000 with a clear vision: to improve access to high-quality English learning materials, especially for students of language institutes.

We began as a small bookstore in San Miguel, Lima, working with major English institutes. Years later, we opened a second store in downtown Lima to expand our reach.

With the pandemic and new technological changes, we transitioned to a fully virtual bookstore, allowing us to serve customers across Peru. Today, we aim to expand even further, reaching students around the world.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has allowed us to grow sustainably while maintaining our core values: trust, quality, and exceptional service.
